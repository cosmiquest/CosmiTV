About CosmiTV Player

CosmiTV is an IPTV Player for Android TV boxes, it's designed to play IPTV Streams from playlists and includes a lot of capabilities such as Rewind, Fast Forward Live TV and schedule recordings.

  • test

Features

  • Pause, rewind, and fast forward Live IP streams
  • IPTV DVR/PVR, Record your favorite shows
  • Full grid modern TV Guide
  • Mark channels as favorites
  • Quick go back to last 10 channel
  • Auto and manual EPG Refresh
  • Support for multiple IPTV M3U playlists and XMLTV guides (local or from internet)
  • Schedule recordings from the Program guide and record it even if you are not watching tv
  • Supports Stalker Portal, Xtream Codes, Free to view Playlists
  • Turn your Android TV to an STB Emulator
  • Home Screen Integration

Note: DVR Recording functionality requires an external drive (SD Card or USB) that is 12GB or larger formatted as device storage.
